Centuries of Craftsmanship

Maheshwari weaving tradition dates back to the 18th century, flourishing under the patronage of Queen Ahilyabai Holkar. Each fabric carries the legacy of master weavers and the architectural beauty of Maheshwar.

Our textiles are characterized by lightweight fabric, reversible borders, and geometric motifs inspired by temple carvings and the rhythmic ghats of the Narmada river.
